What's On

Highlights

👕

Swag Station

Design your own Plank shirt using iron-on transfers on a provided white tee. The 3 most creative designs win prizes — voted on by the whole team (you can't vote for your own).

Both days  ·  Starts Thu 10 am

🧘

Yoga & Mindfulness

A 15-minute guided mindfulness session followed by a 45-minute yoga class with Jeanne. All levels genuinely welcome — this is not an intense class. Bring comfortable clothes.

Thu 11:30 am – 1:00 pm  ·  Suite 118

🚶

Walking Tour

Warren's Montréal — a guided walk through the city that ends at India Rosa for group dinner. Weather-dependent; the McCord Museum is the backup plan if it rains.

Thu 3:00 – 5:30 pm  ·  Wear comfy shoes

🏺

Pottery Workshop

Handbuilding at Atelier Make on Saint-Laurent. You keep what you make — pieces are fired after the event and shipped to anyone not based in Montréal. Aprons provided.

Fri 1:30 – 4:00 pm  ·  Atelier Make

🤝

Community Volunteer

Caroline's community initiative — most likely making bagged lunches for people in need. A chance to give back as a team while we're in town together. Optional but encouraged.

Fri 10:00 am – 12:00 pm  ·  Optional

📸

Team Photos

Fresh headshots and group shots with Adreanne — replacing the old "stock photo" style pics across the site. Maelle is also available for headshots; schedule directly with her.

Thu 10:15 am – 1:00 pm  ·  Plank HQ
