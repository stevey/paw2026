# **PAW 2026**

In 2026, PAW will take place on **Thursday May 28th** & **Friday May 29th**. 


## **Plan for 2 days:**

On Thursday May 28th and Friday May 29th, we’ll arrange for as much of the team as possible to gather in Montreal at the Plank office. 

* **Thursday May 28th** 	  
  * 8am: Office open for everyone   
  * 9am: Breakfast @ office  
  * 10am →  Swag activity intro (station available throughout day — make your own Plank shirt — best 3 shirts win prize)  
  * 10:15am: Townhall roundtable — everyone  
  * 10:15am → after lunch  New photos and group shots (Adreanne)  
  * 11am: Allen and Legal/AI chat/Q\&A  
  * 11:30am: Optional Yoga activity (15m of mindfulness and 45 minutes of yoga)  
  * 12pm: BYO Lunch XL\! Do whatever you want til 3pm   
  * 3pm: Walking tour — Warren's MTL (will end at restaurant)  
  * 5:30pm: Group Dinner @ India Rosa Plateau  
      
* **Friday May 29th**  
  * 8am: Office open for everyone   
  * 9am: Treats (Canoli) and coffee/tea  
  * 9am →  Swag activity continues  
  * 10am: Teams meetings / fun activities / games  
  * 10am til 12pm: Option volunteer activity for Caroline’s community  
  * 12pm: Group Lunch @ office (Selection of Pizza from [https://ilfocolaio.ca](https://ilfocolaio.ca) and Salads from Mandy’s)  
  * 12:30pm: Swag activity vote for top 3 designs (make your own Plank shirt)  
  * 1:30pm: Creative Activity — Pottery  
  * 4pm: Optional wrap up social TBD (somewhere close to Creative Activity)  
    

The spaces in between are for socialing, impromptu discussion, etc. The schedule is purposefully not 100% filled.

Notes on activities: 

* All food at Plank office will have vegan option   
* Swag activity involves cutting out iron on transfers and arranging them on a white t-shirt (your size will be  provided) —3 most creative well design t-shirts (voted by all of us — can't vote for your own shirt) win a prize  
* Townhall will not have a set agenda — just a round table about issues we’re facing, ideas and what to focus on in the rest of 2026\.  
* Option volunteer activity for Caroline’s community will most probably involved making bagged lunches for those in need. We’ve reached out to Caroline to see what is most helpful   
* Optional Yoga activity (15m of mindfulness and 45 minutes of yoga) will be doable by everyone at any level — you can go for just the first part or stay for the full hour — if you’re going for the full hour — suggest a change of clothes appropriate for yoga   
* New photos and group shots (Adreanne) will be to replace our “stock” photos of people together, teams, working, etc.  
* Maelle has offered to take new headshots over the 2 days — if you wish – schedule that directly with her — remember to consider what you’re wearing so you’re happy with the results   
* Allen will be leading a QA/update on AI and legal perspectives for us for 2026 — Q’s for him can be added to \#forum-ai beforehand   
* Walking tour is weather dependant / backup plan is a group visit to the MCCord Museum [https://www.musee-mccord-stewart.ca/en/](https://www.musee-mccord-stewart.ca/en/)  
* India Rosa Plateau group dinner has a full vegan option  
* Walking tour: Please wear walking shoes   
* Creative Activity — Pottery might be a bit messy — they will provide aprons but be aware that you might get a bit dirty (clay washes out of most fabrics). You’ll get to keep anything you make handbuilding (but not throwing) — Steve will pick it up after a few weeks when it’s done. Those far away will have items shipped to them.
